Common Side Effects in Cats

1. Vomiting

Some medications or foods may cause vomiting in cats. Monitor your cat and consult a vet if it persists.

2. Lethargy

Cats may feel tired or less active after certain treatments. Ensure they have a comfortable space to rest.

3. Loss of Appetite

Loss of appetite can occur due to stress, illness, or medication. Encourage hydration and consult a vet if needed.
