Freedom Fighters of India

Mahatma Gandhi

Known as the Father of the Nation, Mahatma Gandhi led India to independence through non-violent civil disobedience.

Bhagat Singh

A revolutionary leader, Bhagat Singh fought against British rule and became a symbol of youth and courage.

Rani Lakshmibai

The Queen of Jhansi, Rani Lakshmibai, was a key figure in the Indian Rebellion of 1857, known for her bravery and leadership.
